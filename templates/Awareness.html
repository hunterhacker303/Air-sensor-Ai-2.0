Understanding The Invisible

Air pollution is the single largest environmental health risk. Learn about what you are breathing.

Particulate Matter 2.5

Fine inhalable particles 2.5 micrometers or smaller. They penetrate deep into the lungs and enter the bloodstream, causing cardiovascular and respiratory problems.

Particulate Matter 10

Larger inhalable particles such as dust, pollen, and mold. They irritate eyes, nose, throat, and reduce breathing comfort.

Volatile Organic Compounds

VOCs come from paints, chemicals, cleaners, and plastics. Long-term exposure affects the liver, kidneys, and nervous system.

Health Impacts

  • Respiratory Disease Aggravates asthma, increases coughing, and decreases lung function.
  • Cardiovascular Damage Linked to heart attacks, stroke risk, and irregular heart rhythms.
